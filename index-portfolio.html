About Me

Hello. My name is Dheepa Sivakumaran. I have designed this website with the intent of improving my communication with the public in a digital environment. My academic background is in English literature. I am fascinated by how writers in various genres use language creatively. I am new to coding, and have started learning languages fundamental to the web. In turn, this has sparked further interest in understanding how programmers use computers to translate web languages in order to facilitate digital communication.

I enjoy reading both fiction and non-fiction, mass media, music, and keeping up-to-date on all current events. I also have a strong interest in different kinds of journalistic writing. I can be contacted at dheepa.siva@gmail.com.
